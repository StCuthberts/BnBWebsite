St Cuthberts of Lyme Bed & Breakfast Rooms

Room 1

King sized hand made brass bed. Ensuite wet room. Flat screen TV, clock radio with alarm and hair dryer. Small fridge with fresh milk. Tea and coffee making facilities. Juliet balcony with views across the garden to Lyme Bay.

Room 2

King sized hand made iron bed. A single bed can also be put in this room, to make it a twin or for a child. Ensuite wet room. Flat screen TV, clock radio with alarm and hair dryer. Small fridge with fresh milk. Tea and coffee making facilities. Juliet balcony with views across a well established garden.

Room 3

King sized bed. Ensuite wet room. This room has the extra advantage of a sitting room with a large sofa and 2 arm chairs. Flat screen TV’s, clock radio with alarm, hair dryer and ironing board. Small fridge with fresh milk. Tea and coffee making facilities. Juliet balcony with views across the garden to Lyme Bay and Uplyme.

  

Tariffs
Room 1 - With 2 Sharing is £95 per night and breakfast.
Room 2 - With 2 Sharing is £95 per night and breakfast, for 3 sharing is £110 per night and breakfast.
Room 3 - With 2 sharing is £105 per night and breakfast (its well worth the extra £10).
There is a discount for single occupancy.
Please note we DO NOT take any card payments, Cash only.

Arrival is by arrangement. Let us know approximately your arrival time and we will plan our day around you. Rooms can sometimes be ready earlier than you might think.
Rooms must be vacated by 10:30am at the latest on the day of departure.

We take a lot of our bookings on trust, but if you wish to send a deposit, a cheque for £45 will secure the room what ever length your stay might be. Any cancellations PLEASE let us know as soon as possible so that we can try and relet the room keeping our loses to a minimum.

We sometimes take single night bookings, just email or call and we will see if we have availability.

Please be aware that there are a number to steps though the garden to get to the front door. Therefore we are unable to take anyone who has difficulty with stairs or in a wheel chair, sorry.
