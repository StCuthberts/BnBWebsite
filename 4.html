St Cuthberts Breakfast

At St Cuthberts, breakfast is ordered the night before. You will be given an order card where you can choose exactly what you would like. Customers can choose what time they would like their breakfast which is any time between 7.30am and 9.30am. This means we can make your breakfast to order, so that it comes out freshly cooked for optimum enjoyment.
